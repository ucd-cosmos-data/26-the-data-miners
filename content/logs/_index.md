# Mini Project #1
## What Kind of Music Does Cluster 11 Enjoy?

## Introduction

This mini activity collected survey responses from students about their background and music preferences. The survey asked about gender, grade level, race, favorite artist, and opinion on the best Drake album. Our goal was to summarize the responses and identify clear patterns in the data.

## Dataset Overview

- Total responses: **19**
- Survey date: **July 7, 2026**
- Main categories analyzed:
  - Gender
  - Grade level
  - Race
  - Favorite artist
  - Best Drake album

## Respondent Demographics

### Gender

| Gender | Count | Percentage |
|---|---:|---:|
| Male | 14 | 73.7% |
| Female | 5 | 26.3% |

Most respondents were male, making up nearly three-fourths of the survey.

### Grade Level

| Grade | Count | Percentage |
|---|---:|---:|
| Senior | 10 | 52.6% |
| Junior | 9 | 47.4% |

The grade distribution was very balanced, with almost equal numbers of juniors and seniors.

### Race

| Race | Count | Percentage |
|---|---:|---:|
| Asian | 9 | 47.4% |
| South Asian | 8 | 42.1% |
| White | 2 | 10.5% |

Most respondents identified as Asian or South Asian, together making up almost 90% of the group.

## Music Preferences

### Favorite Artist

| Artist | Count | Percentage |
|---|---:|---:|
| Drake | 5 | 26.3% |
| The Weeknd | 5 | 26.3% |
| Olivia Rodrigo | 3 | 15.8% |
| Lady Gaga | 2 | 10.5% |
| Travis Scott | 2 | 10.5% |
| Billie Eilish | 1 | 5.3% |
| Kendrick Lamar | 1 | 5.3% |

Drake and The Weeknd were tied as the most popular favorite artists. Olivia Rodrigo was the next most common choice.

### Best Drake Album

| Album | Count | Percentage |
|---|---:|---:|
| Views (2016) | 4 | 21.1% |
| Certified Lover Boy (2021) | 3 | 15.8% |
| ICEMAN (2026) | 3 | 15.8% |
| Scorpion (2018) | 2 | 10.5% |
| Take Care (2011) | 2 | 10.5% |
| Honestly, Nevermind (2022) | 1 | 5.3% |
| Nothing Was the Same (2013) | 1 | 5.3% |
| For All the Dogs (2023) | 1 | 5.3% |
| If You're Reading This It's Too Late | 1 | 5.3% |
| Thank Me Later (2010) | 1 | 5.3% |

The most selected Drake album was **Views (2016)**, followed by **Certified Lover Boy (2021)** and **ICEMAN (2026)**.

## Key Insights

- The survey group was mostly male, but grade level was evenly split between juniors and seniors.
- The racial makeup was mostly Asian and South Asian.
- Drake and The Weeknd were the strongest favorite artist choices.
- **Views (2016)** was the top choice for best Drake album.
- Female respondents showed stronger interest in artists like Olivia Rodrigo and Lady Gaga, while male respondents more often chose Drake and The Weeknd.

## Conclusion

This mini survey shows that the group has a strong preference for popular hip-hop and pop artists, especially Drake and The Weeknd. While the sample size is small, the data still reveals useful patterns about the music taste and demographics of the respondents.
